With over 15 years experience and hundreds of thousands of images in her archives, Susan has mastered photography with mainly natural light. Her scenic and product imagery are a natural extension of her photographic skills. She has had her work appear in leading publications such as Harpers Bazaar, Red Book, Orlando Style, DuPont Registry, Interview Magazine, Flair and many others. She is highly proficient with studio settings and works with the best technology. Her skill sets include wide range of camera use, natural lighting, studio lighting and the use of photo enhancing software. Susan collaborates with a team of experienced stylists, make-up artists and art directors. She has the ability to bring out the best in her talent. Her studio is within minutes of sunny Florida beaches and great many natural settings. Stylists include:Tina Jackson, Pamela Bloomgren, Michael Bassolino. Hair and Make-up artists include: Lindsay Matacchiero and Bill Baker.
